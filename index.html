Research

My main research area is (homological) algebra, with connections to deformation theory and noncommutative algebraic geometry.

More specifically, I am currently working on developing a deformation theory for (enhanced) triangulated categories with the goal of understanding the relation between deformations of a dg-algebra and deformations of its derived category. If you are interested in the topic, here are the slides from a presentation I did in Antwerp introducing the problem in general terms and here is a poster that explains my recent research in more detail.
