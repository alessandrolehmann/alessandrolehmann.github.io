Research

My main research area is homological algebra, with connections to deformation theory and noncommutative algebraic geometry. I am also interested in the interactions between algebraic geometry and higher category theory.

More specifically, my current PhD project revolves around understanding curved deformations of differential graded algebras through their derived categories. If you are interested in the topic, here are the slides from a presentation I did in Antwerp introducing the problem in general terms.
